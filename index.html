How to Play
  1. Players need to get all of their tokens to the home space first.
  2. RED begins first, then GREEN. And game begins alternately.
  3. Before you can move a token, you will need to roll a six. If you don’t roll a six, then the play will instantly move to the next player.
  4. ALL FOUR CORNERS ARE SAFE SQUARES.
  5. If you land on the same square as another player’s token at the end of your roll, you can capture it. Your opponent will then need to roll a six again to bring it back onto the board.
  6. If you get 3 CONSECUTIVE 6's, then the play will instantly move to the next player.
